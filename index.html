Monumental display.

Series 10 is a major milestone for Apple Watch. It features our biggest and most advanced display yet,3 showing more information onscreen than ever. With Apple’s first wide-angle OLED Display, the screen is brighter when viewed from an angle, making it easier to read with a quick glance.

Sleek form.

Series 10 is our thinnest watch ever. To make this possible, we re-engineered nearly every aspect of it, including the SiP, Digital Crown, speaker, front crystal, and antenna. It all comes together to create a watch that’s packed full of innovation.

Series 10 has the largest screen area of any Apple Watch. It has up to 75% more screen area than Series 3, up to 30% more screen area than Series 4, 5, 6, and SE, and up to 9% more screen area than Series 7, 8, and 9. So you can see and do even more.
